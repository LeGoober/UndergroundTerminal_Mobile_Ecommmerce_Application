The platform

Built for the three sides of the luxury trade.

High-value goods move on trust. Underground Terminal verifies every account, signs every session, and gives each side of a transaction exactly the controls it needs — nothing shared that shouldn't be.

How it works

Four moves from listing to landed.

The platform runs the same path every time — that's what makes it auditable.

01 · LIST

Catalogue

Suppliers publish goods with pricing, imagery and opening stock. Every listing belongs to a verified account.

02 · VERIFY

Clearance

Buyers sign in with email or Google. Sessions are token-signed end to end — no anonymous trade.

03 · TRANSACT

Settlement

Orders execute inside the platform. Stock decrements at the moment of sale, on both sides of the deal.

04 · TRACK

Manifest

Consignments appear on the live manifest until delivery is confirmed — buyer and supplier watch the same board.
